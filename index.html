Physical Therapy

Physical therapy is a treatment method that helps restore movement and manage pain through exercises, manual therapy, and rehabilitation techniques.

Heat Therapy

Heat therapy uses warmth to relax muscles, improve circulation, and alleviate pain or stiffness in the body.

Neuro Physiotherapy

Neurotherapy focuses on treating neurological conditions by enhancing brain function and promoting nervous system healing through targeted exercises and therapies.
