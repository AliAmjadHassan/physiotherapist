My Services

Stroke Rehabilitation

Stroke rehabilitation focuses on helping individuals regain strength, coordination, and functional abilities after a stroke. It includes exercises, mobility training, and balance improvement techniques to promote independence.

Dry Needling

Dry needling is a technique used to treat muscle pain and tightness. By inserting fine needles into trigger points, the therapy relieves tension, improves mobility, and reduces pain in affected muscles.

Cardiovascular Physical Therapy

Cardiovascular physical therapy aids patients in recovering from heart conditions by improving stamina, strength, and overall cardiovascular function through structured exercise programs.

Heat Therapy

Heat therapy is used to relax muscles, improve blood circulation, and ease pain. It's commonly applied to treat chronic pain, muscle spasms, and stiffness in various conditions.

Range Of Motion Therapy

Range of motion therapy helps to maintain or improve joint flexibility by encouraging movement through specific exercises, especially after injury or surgery.

Vestibular Rehabilitation

Vestibular rehabilitation is a specialized therapy to reduce dizziness and improve balance for individuals suffering from inner ear disorders and vertigo.

Amputation Rehabilitation

Amputation rehabilitation assists individuals in adapting to life post-amputation. It focuses on strengthening, mobility training, and prosthetic limb education to restore independence.

Chiropractic

Chiropractic care focuses on diagnosing and treating musculoskeletal problems, particularly through manual adjustments of the spine to alleviate pain and improve function.

KINESIO Taping

Kinesio taping involves applying special tape to muscles and joints to provide support, reduce pain, and enhance healing while allowing a full range of motion.

Low Back Pain

Therapy for low back pain involves targeted exercises, posture correction, and pain-relieving techniques to strengthen the core and alleviate discomfort.

Manual Therapy

Manual therapy includes hands-on techniques such as soft tissue mobilization and joint manipulation to relieve pain, improve mobility, and restore function.

Neuro Physiotherapy

Neuro physiotherapy aids patients with neurological conditions like Parkinson's, MS, and stroke by improving movement, coordination, and muscle control through specialized exercises.

Pediatric Physiotherapy

Pediatric physiotherapy helps children with physical challenges, focusing on improving motor skills, strength, coordination, and functional abilities tailored to their developmental needs.

Specialist In Physiotherapy Consulting

Specialist physiotherapy consulting offers expert advice and tailored rehabilitation plans for complex musculoskeletal or neurological conditions, ensuring optimal recovery outcomes.

Sports Injuries Physiotherapy

Sports injuries physiotherapy helps athletes recover from injuries and prevent future ones by improving strength, flexibility, and proper movement patterns.

Neurological Physical Therapy

Neurological physical therapy aids in the recovery of individuals with conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nerves, focusing on restoring movement and coordination.

Geriatric Physical Therapy

Geriatric physical therapy focuses on improving the mobility, balance, and strength of older adults, helping them maintain independence and manage age-related conditions.

Soft Tissue Mobilization

Soft tissue mobilization involves applying hands-on techniques to relax muscles, break up scar tissue, and improve circulation, reducing pain and improving flexibility.

Acupuncture

Acupuncture involves inserting fine needles into specific points on the body to relieve pain, reduce inflammation, and promote healing through traditional Chinese medicine principles.

Gynae And Obstetrics Physiotherapy

This physiotherapy addresses conditions related to women's reproductive health, including prenatal and postnatal care, pelvic floor dysfunction, and managing pain during pregnancy.

Lacto Therapy

Lacto therapy helps breastfeeding mothers with issues like lactation pain, milk supply challenges, and breastfeeding techniques, promoting both mother and baby’s well-being.

Magnetic Therapy, Bio Lamp And Manual Therapy

This therapy combines magnetic therapy, bio lamp healing, and manual techniques to alleviate pain, reduce inflammation, and promote natural healing of muscles and joints.

Musculoskeletal Disorders

Physiotherapy for musculoskeletal disorders focuses on treating conditions affecting muscles, bones, and joints, such as arthritis, sprains, and back pain, aiming to restore function and reduce pain.

Orthopedic Physio Therapist

Orthopedic physiotherapists specialize in treating injuries and conditions related to the skeletal system, focusing on rehabilitation and recovery following fractures, surgeries, or trauma.

Special Children Physiotherapy

This physiotherapy service caters to children with developmental challenges, helping improve motor function, balance, and coordination, supporting their growth and independence.

Spine Manipulation And Mobilization

Spine manipulation and mobilization involve hands-on techniques to improve spinal alignment and reduce pain, improving range of motion and reducing pressure on nerves.
